Idea

Why choosing Lunar?

Lunar is a software for sharing renewably produced energy. We are enabling a peer-to-peer marketplace between producers and consumers of energy. We encourage privat persons to use solar panels and earn $LNR coins for their produced energy. Lunar is decentralized meaning there is no central authority controlling the energy flow. Lunar is using a microgrid (offshore energy network).

Our vision is to create an ecosystem where houses turn into smart homes. They share renewable energy with each other and contribute to a brighter future. Our ongrowing demand of energy will keep rising so it’s time to decentralize this market and work towards a safe and green future for us, for our children and for the next generations.

Smart meters measure the production and consumption of a device and store it into the network.
The Lunar Rail is a distributed ledger which is AI-driven and super fast due to its special data structure.
The technology works on the microgrid which is a self supplying energy network functioning independently.
Each energy which is generated by a renewable source redeems a CO2 certificat on the network.
